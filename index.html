SACCO APP EXPERIENCE


This is a mobile web demo of a group investing app that would work for a Savings and Credits Cooperative (SACCO). The app features group investing and harvesting(credits) and ability to create small vestings and get investment from friends. You can create taglines for vestings and the trending ones on your timeline.
